Watch your favorite movies, listen to your favorite music albums (on the Internet, your PC, tablet, phone or stored in SmartBox) and watch the best free multimedia content in your language and right on the box. Enjoy the full experience of a Home Theater and SmartTV without replacing your current LCD or plasma TV. It's simple to use and most of the setup is already done when you receive the device, without the hassle of building it and installing the software yourself.

The current set-top boxes offer content from services that are not available outside the USA. Also, most of them are actually small PC's or Android devices, so interacting with them using a remote control is cumbersome. Also, it is difficult to add new channels. The idea is to bring you the best selection of free and available multimedia content, both local and worldwide, directly to your couch. With tons of videos, podcast and radio stations in the Internet, it is hard to choose. But you can follow our editor's choice or you can follow the recommendations of the SmartBox community. And you can add your own channels too and share the SmartBox community, using your Facebook or Twitter account.

FAQ

What Channels are available?
The list of default providers is still in process. So far, it will include the most common (Netflix, Hulu, Crackle, Revision3 and some International and Regional Channels). But if you have an Internet radio station, Streaming TV channel, podcast or videopodcast to recommend don't hesitate to contact us!. If you preorder this product, you will receive by e-mail a form with the additional options you want and if the streams are compatible with our device we will made the setup for you. However, you can change the setup manually any time.
Can I play games on my SmartBox?
Yes. However, the kind of games that you can play is limited by SmartBox hardware and the publishing limitations of game developers.
How much does it cost?
The goal is to sell each item for USD$150. However, this is an estimated price and a more detailed cost analysis is required in order to establish a final price. Please register in order to receive updates. If you are interested in the product, but you want to wait until we have a final price, please support it on Twitter or make a donation.
