Watch your favorite movies, listen to your favorite music albums (on Internet, your PC, tablet, phone or stored in SmartBox) and watch the best free multimedia content in your language and right on the box. The current set-top boxes offer content from services that are not available outside the USA. Also, most of them are actually small PC's or Android devices, so interacting with them using a remote control is cumbersome. Also, it is difficult to add new channels. We have made the best selection of free and available multimedia content, both local and worldwide, directly to your couch.
